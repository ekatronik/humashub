International Seminar Academic Integrity

Global Perspectives on Islamic Higher Education and Digital Transformation

This comprehensive seminar brought together leading scholars, university administrators, and policy makers from across Southeast Asia to discuss the integration of digital technologies within the framework of Islamic higher education. The event aimed to establish new collaborative networks and share best practices for maintaining academic excellence while embracing modern pedagogical tools. Keynote speeches addressed the challenges of cultural preservation in the digital age, followed by focused roundtable discussions on curriculum modernization.
